About us

My name is Karina Lopez I am 32 years old, I was born and raised in Boyle heights. I decided to do a podcast to try and help other women like me who struggle with the craziness life throws at you. Including motherhood, work-life, relationships, and trying to stay sane all at once. I thought that if I share my story and past experiences I could help others get through their day to day struggles. I came up with the name “selfluv” because it’s something that took me years to learn how to do. .

Now at 32, I have learned to love my self enough to where I am no longer settling for less. I will no longer accept a dead-end job, put a man before me, and allow people to treat me any less than what I deserve. I am not a doctor, psychiatrist, or psychic. I don’t have all the answers and I don’t think I do, I speak only on past experiences what I’ve seen and what I know. I have learned to value my self, and you will too because you should always come first. Follow me on my journey as I continue to figure this life out, and know that as long as I’m around you will not walk alone
